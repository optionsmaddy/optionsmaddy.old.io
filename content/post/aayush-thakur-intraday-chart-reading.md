- There are only two price observation
	1. Continuation of momentum
	2. Reversal of momentum
- Opening
	- Gap Up
	- Sideways
	- Gap down
- See pattern till 10 O clock. Is it breaking highs or lows.
	- Buy swing.
	- Sell swing.
